Investigation Overview¶

In this dataset, I would like to shed some light on the borrower's charachteristics and how this can affect the properties of the loan borrowed.

Dataset Overview¶

This data set contains 113,937 loans with 81 variables on each loan, including loan amount, borrower rate (or interest rate), current loan status, borrower income, and many others.

1. The Distribution of Borrower's Income Range:¶

Most of the borrowers have an income ranging from 25k to 50 k and from 50 k to 75k dollars. Then, the following categories are from 75 k to 100 k and more than 100 k dollars.

3. The relationship between Term, Loan original amount and Income Range:¶

The interaction between the three variables was really interesting. Although most of the borrowers income range was from 25 to 75k dollars, the major income range for 12 month borrowers was 75 to 100k dollars while for 36 and 60 month loans, the major income range was more than 100k dollars.
